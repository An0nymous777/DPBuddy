EK=Kinetic energy Unit: J (N·m = kg·m2·s-2)
Definition/note:
 •Kinectic energy is the energy of a body due

to kinetic energy (ΔKE+ΔPE=0).

Definition/note:

the weight of an object resting on it.

 •Inclined object: R = mgcos(θ).
 •Always act perpendicular to the
contact surface.
